Executive Summary

Recommendation: Proceed to V2 development with controlled V1 demonstration program — subject to security hardening and insurance confirmation.

FollowMeWagon is a smartphone-controlled, GPS-synchronized autonomous follow wagon targeting the premium golf caddy market. Unlike all current competitors — which require proprietary beacons, wearables, or handheld remotes — FollowMeWagon operates entirely via standard smartphone browser, delivering a differentiated, hardware-light user experience at a $1,750 retail price within validated customer willingness-to-pay of $1,600–$1,900.

The golf equipment TAM is $9.5B (HIGH confidence). The autonomous caddy sub-segment is estimated at $320M growing at 13.6% CAGR (LOW confidence — requires primary analyst validation before capital commitment). Unit economics are strong: 48.6% gross margin at 1,000-unit scale, with break-even achievable at 33–38 units. Capital required: $35,000–$50,000 base case; $60,000–$80,000 stress case.

Top 3 risks requiring resolution before commercial deployment:

  • R1V1 has no integrated obstacle detection — untethered public deployment is prohibited until HC-SR04 is integrated and field-validated.
  • R2The WiFi command interface is unauthenticated — any device on the same network can issue movement commands, creating direct physical safety and liability exposure.
  • R3Insurance cost uncertainty ($3,000–$25,000/year range) is the single assumption most capable of restructuring the financial model; specialty insurer quotes are required before capital commitment.

Key Findings

1
Unit economics are commercially viable. At 1,000-unit scale, total cost per unit is $900 against a $1,750 retail price, yielding 48.6% gross margin — sufficient to absorb fixed overhead and fund continued development.

V1 is a demonstration asset, not a shippable product. Missing obstacle detection, an unauthenticated control interface, and GPS accuracy of 2–5 meters restrict V1 to supervised, open-environment use with signed participant waivers only.
3
Competitive positioning is genuinely differentiated. No current autonomous caddy competitor operates via standard smartphone without a proprietary wearable or beacon — this is a defensible and communicable market advantage.
4
The V2 upgrade path is low-cost and technically straightforward. Total V2 incremental hardware cost is under $200 per unit, with most critical components already in inventory or commercially available off-the-shelf.
5
Three financial assumptions require external validation before capital commitment. Sub-TAM size, insurance premiums, and marketing CAC are currently LOW confidence and materially affect viability projections.

Cybersecurity Assessment

WiFi Command Channel
Flask interface is currently unauthenticated. Mutual TLS, HTTPS enforcement, and signed command tokens are mandatory before any commercial deployment or public demonstration.
GPS Spoofing
Low-cost SDR devices (~$20) can inject false position signals. Mitigation: implement position plausibility filtering — reject inputs exceeding maximum speed and distance-delta thresholds.
Unauthorized Access
No open-network operation permitted. Pi must operate as a secured hotspot with WPA3 encryption and device allowlisting before field deployment.
Firmware Integrity
Signed firmware updates required. No unauthenticated OTA update path may be exposed in production builds.
Location Data Privacy
No persistent GPS coordinate logging without explicit user opt-in. Data minimization policy required at launch.
Compliance Baseline
OWASP IoT Top 10 and NIST SP 800-213 are the baseline compliance framework. All five vectors above must be closed before commercial deployment.

Conditions for Reversal

Upgrades recommendation to Strong Proceed

Specialty insurer quotes confirm product liability premiums at or below $5,000/year, validating the base-case financial model and making the stress scenario a planning buffer rather than a threat.

Independent primary analyst data confirms the autonomous caddy sub-TAM at or above $250M with CAGR above 10%, substantiating the beachhead revenue ceiling.

Marketing CAC validates at or below $200/unit through the golf influencer test, preserving margin targets at moderate scale.

Downgrades recommendation to Pause or Stop

Product liability insurance quotes exceed $15,000/year across multiple specialty carriers, making the moderate-scenario financial model unprofitable at current pricing without a retail price increase above validated customer willingness-to-pay.

Obstacle detection integration reveals fundamental control system instability that cannot be resolved within the V2 development budget, eliminating the path to public deployment.

A prior art search or freedom-to-operate analysis identifies blocking patents that structurally constrain the smartphone-native follow architecture, removing the primary competitive differentiator and reducing FollowMeWagon to a feature-equivalent commodity.
